# Header/Footer Theme Notes

This project is now a full Extra Mile Mechanic website.

## Design direction

- Primary brand color: racing red (`#e31e24`)
- Support palette: black, white, and slate neutrals
- Tone: direct, trustworthy, and practical

## Core UX goals

- Keep call and WhatsApp actions prominent across pages
- Keep navigation simple and mobile friendly
- Keep service and trust messaging consistent

## Key components

- `components/Header.tsx`: logo, nav links, top contact strip
- `components/Footer.tsx`: quick links plus direct contact details
- `components/PageHeader.tsx`: consistent inner-page hero block
- `components/ContactForm.tsx`: Formspree enquiry/booking form

## Content focus

- Honest pricing and transparent diagnostics
- Free extra checks after each completed job
- Long-term relationship messaging and customer trust
